Home ICU Setup in 4 Easy Steps

We at HDU believe that patient safety is the most critical determinant, thus we have developed a 4-step process to ensure that our patient safely transferred to home.

Free Patient Assessment

Get a free patient assessment by experienced HDU medical team at Hospital.

Submission of Care Plan for Patient

Get a detailed transparent care plan having list of medical device & Service required to manage the patient at home.

Equipment Testing & Installation

We test all medical devices at our centre before installation at home. Testing of Ventilator & BiPAP device while patient at hospital before moving to home.

Shifting the Patient

After installation process of all medical devices at home gets complete Our Nursing Manager will assist and move the patient to the Home ICU Care.
